# Budget Cycle

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Development  
**Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Product Vision

## Vision

Empower people to make intentional financial decisions by planning every income cycle before spending a single peso.

Budget Cycle transforms every paycheck into a clear financial plan, helping users understand where every unit of money should go before it is actually spent.

---

# Mission

Budget Cycle exists to help people build financial awareness through intentional income distribution.

Instead of tracking expenses after they happen, Budget Cycle encourages users to assign a purpose to 100% of their income as soon as they receive it.

The objective is to reduce financial stress, increase confidence, and create long-term financial habits through structured planning.

---

# Problem Statement

Most personal finance applications focus on recording expenses.

This approach has several limitations:

- Users only analyze what already happened.
- Financial planning becomes reactive instead of proactive.
- Income is often spent without a predefined purpose.
- Budget categories usually become disconnected from real financial goals.

People rarely ask:

> "How should I distribute this income?"

Instead they ask:

> "Where did my money go?"

Budget Cycle changes that mindset.

---

# Product Philosophy

Budget Cycle is built around one fundamental principle:

> Every peso should have a purpose before it is spent.

The application is designed to help users make intentional financial decisions every time they receive income.

The system does not judge spending.

The system encourages planning.

---

# Core Principles

## 1. Income First

Everything begins with an income cycle.

No planning exists without income.

The Cycle is the central entity of the entire product.

---

## 2. Every Peso Has a Job

100% of every income must be assigned to a destination.

Unassigned money creates uncertainty.

Assigned money creates clarity.

---

## 3. Planning Before Spending

Budget Cycle focuses on future decisions.

It is not an expense tracker.

It is a planning system.

---

## 4. Simplicity Over Complexity

Financial planning should be understandable by anyone.

Users should not need accounting knowledge to use the application.

---

## 5. Flexibility

Every user manages money differently.

Default Buckets provide guidance.

Custom Buckets allow personalization.

---

## 6. Financial Awareness

The objective is not merely to calculate percentages.

The objective is to increase financial awareness.

The application should continuously help users understand:

- where their money is going
- why it is going there
- whether their distribution aligns with their goals

---

# What Budget Cycle IS

Budget Cycle is:

- an Income Planning System
- a Budget Distribution Tool
- a Financial Awareness Platform
- a Personal Planning Assistant
- a Money Allocation Framework

---

# What Budget Cycle IS NOT

Budget Cycle is not:

- an accounting platform
- an ERP
- an invoicing application
- a bookkeeping tool
- a banking application
- an expense tracker
- a tax management system

---

# Target Audience

Budget Cycle is designed for individuals who:

- receive recurring income
- want to improve financial discipline
- prefer proactive budgeting
- seek clarity about their money
- are building wealth intentionally

The application is suitable for:

- employees
- freelancers
- entrepreneurs
- students with recurring income
- families managing household finances

# Success Metrics

The product succeeds when users:

- consistently create new income cycles
- distribute 100% of every income
- maintain healthy financial habits
